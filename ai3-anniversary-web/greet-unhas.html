Greeting Messages


Dr. Dwia Ariestina Pohululu
Vice Rector for External Affairs, Hasanuddin University

I am very pleased to congratulate Asian Internet Interconnection Initiatives (AI3) and School on Internet (SOI) project for the 10th and 5th anniversary. I also congratulate all the partners of AI3 and SOI Asia project for the contributions.

I have learned that SOI-Asia Project has had tremendous contribution to enhance knowledge and skill of students and academic staffs of Hasanuddin University on various disciplines. The SOI-Asia Project has broadened the horizon of student and academic staff on the application of Information and Communication Technologies (ICT). I am very sure that project has had created strong basis for the development of ICT at Hasanuddin University and also at other partner universities. I strongly believe that AI3 and SOI-Asia Project have had generated invaluable benefits to all the partners.

In conclusion, I once again congratulate AI3 for its 10th Anniversary and SOI-Asia Project for its 5th Anniversary. Salute for all of the accomplishments. I wish a great success for the future development and thank you for giving Hasanuddin University an opportunity to involve in the project.

Thank You.
